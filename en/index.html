BarePaper is a handwriting app for iPad with one job: writing.

It feels like paper. The canvas never ends. The screen stays out of your way. When everything that interrupts your thinking is stripped away and only writing is left, the things you've kept buried start to surface.

That's the space BarePaper was built to be.

Three things writing uncovers

01

Meet your unfiltered self

No one's reading. Nothing needs to look good. When you stop editing yourself, feelings you didn't know you had find their way onto the page.

02

Watch ideas connect

Add a thought beside a thought, under a thought, anywhere. With no edges and no pages to turn, scattered notes start to become one line of thinking.

03

Find ideas you didn't know you had

You think as you write — so things that weren't in your head yet show up on the page. Writing doesn't just record thought. It creates it.

Five deliberate choices

Feels like paper

A paper-textured background and clean, simple strokes. Nothing decorative ever made it in — by design.

A pared-down screen

No toolbars full of features you'll never use. Your writing time stays yours.

A canvas without edges

A border is a limit on thinking. The canvas keeps going as long as your thoughts do.

A timer for writing meditation

Set a time, sink in, and write until it gently lets you know. A small feature for treating writing like meditation.

Take it with you as PDF

Export your pages as PDFs and keep them anywhere you like.
